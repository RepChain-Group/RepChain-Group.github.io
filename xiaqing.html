    • Biography

    I am a research assistant in ISCAS. My research interests include consensus protocol, smart contract and blockchain application. In addition, I also work in the RepChain blockchain group, dedicated to the development of an open-source, decentralized blockchain platform RepChain. Based on the platform, our group has implemented various blockchain applications, including e-government, supply chain management, etc.

    I received my Ph.D. degree in 2022 at ISCAS, and earned my Bachelor's degree in 2016 at Central University of Finance and Economics (CUFE).
